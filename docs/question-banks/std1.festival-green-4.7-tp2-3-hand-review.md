## Authoring notes for the reviewer (issue #20)

- **One picture = one value, always (AC3).** The pictograph spec has no
  scale field at all — a scaled pictograph is unauthorable in the core DSL
  (the invalid example in schema-v2.md proves the rejection). Most/least is
  asked as "how many does the most/least have" so every answer stays
  numeric; comparisons are differences and ✓/✗ judgments.
- The three boards are coherent little worlds: the harvest board (6/4/8),
  the sorting stall (fruit 5+3 vs vegetables 4+2), and the green's creature
  board (7/5/3). Distractors lean on wrong-row reads (`next-vs-between`),
  the flipped operation, and the compared operands.
